About Us

The DSC chapter of University Institute of Technology RGPV, Bhopal promotes and felicitates a community of like minded students who are problem solvers and eager learners from different subject backgrounds, who are interested in development in multiple technologies such as Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Web Development, Android Development, and Cloud Computing, amongst a plethora of others. We aim to create a ‘peer to peer learning environment’, alongside a conducive and open environment for development in technologies that are at the forefront of the tech and Information Technology industries, and encourage collaboration amongst students from the university.
